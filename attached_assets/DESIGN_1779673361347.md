## Brand & Style

The design system is anchored in the concept of "Luminous Mentorship." It targets sophisticated creators and learners who value intellectual growth within a calm, distraction-free environment. The brand personality is nurturing yet professional, evoking the feeling of a sun-drenched library or a high-end atelier.

The design style is **Warm Minimalism with Tactile Softness**. It avoids the clinical coldness of traditional SaaS by using a parchment-inspired palette and organic forms. Visual hierarchy is established through subtle tonal shifts and generous whitespace rather than aggressive borders or loud colors. The emotional response should be one of "focused serenity"—where the UI recedes to let the educational content and human connection shine.

## Colors

The palette is a curated selection of "Earth-and-Light" tones. 
- **The Canvas:** The primary background (`#F6F1EA`) acts as a warm, off-white parchment.
- **The Layers:** Secondary backgrounds provide depth for sidebars and headers, while the Card background (`#FFF9F2`) provides a subtle "lift" that feels clean and inviting.
- **The Accent:** The Gold-ochre (`#C8A96B`) is used sparingly for primary actions, success states, and premium "creator" badges, signifying value and quality.
- **Typography:** Contrast is maintained through deep chocolate browns rather than pure blacks to keep the reading experience soft on the eyes.

## Typography

This design system utilizes a dual-font strategy to balance modernity with approachability. 

**Manrope** is used for headlines to provide a structured, professional, and slightly technical edge. It ensures the platform feels like a serious tool for monetization and learning. 

**Be Vietnam Pro** is used for body text and labels. Its slightly wider apertures and friendly character shapes make long-form course content easy to digest and social interactions feel warm and conversational. 

Maintain generous line-heights (minimum 1.6 for body) to preserve the "breathable" aesthetic. Use `text_secondary` for body text to reduce harsh contrast against the warm backgrounds.

## Layout & Spacing

The layout philosophy follows a **Fixed-Fluid Hybrid** model. Content is housed within a maximum container width of 1200px to ensure line lengths remain readable on ultra-wide monitors.

**Spacing Rhythm:** 
We use a base-4 scale but prioritize "Oversized Padding" to create a premium feel. 
- **Desktop:** 12-column grid with 24px gutters. Sections should be separated by `xxl` (64px) vertical spacing.
- **Mobile:** Single column with 16px side margins.
- **Breathability:** Cards and containers should never feel cramped. Use a minimum of `lg` (24px) internal padding for all content containers. 

**Organic Shapes:** 
Incorporate SVG background shapes (blobs or soft waves) with 3-5% opacity using the `secondary_color_hex`. These should sit behind content layers, breaking the rigidity of the grid and adding an "organic" flow to the user journey.

## Elevation & Depth

Depth in this design system is created through **Tonal Layering** and **Diffusion**, rather than harsh shadows.

- **Level 0 (Base):** `background_main` (#F6F1EA).
- **Level 1 (Cards/Surface):** `background_card` (#FFF9F2). This level uses a very soft, high-spread shadow: `0px 10px 30px rgba(43, 33, 24, 0.04)`.
- **Level 2 (Hover/Active):** When a user interacts with a card, the shadow tightens and deepens slightly: `0px 15px 40px rgba(43, 33, 24, 0.08)`.
- **Level 3 (Modals/Overlays):** These use a backdrop blur (8px) combined with the primary card background to create a "frosted parchment" effect.

**Borders:** Use subtle 1px borders only when necessary for accessibility (e.g., input fields). Border color should be a 10% opacity version of `text_primary`.

## Shapes

The shape language is dominated by high-radius curves to reinforce the "Warm/Soft" brand pillars. 

- **Standard Components:** Buttons, inputs, and small widgets use a **16px** (1rem) radius.
- **Primary Containers:** Content cards, course modules, and profile headers use a **24px** (1.5rem) radius.
- **Avatars:** Should be strictly circular to contrast against the softened-square nature of the cards.

## Components

### Buttons
- **Primary:** Background `accent`, text `white` or `text_primary` (high contrast). 16px roundedness. No border.
- **Secondary:** Background `transparent`, border 1px `accent`, text `accent`.
- **Tertiary/Ghost:** No background or border. Text `text_secondary`. Use for less important actions.

### Cards
Cards are the primary vehicle for content. They must always use the `background_card` hex and 24px roundedness. Internal padding should be `lg` (24px).

### Input Fields
- Background: `background_secondary`.
- Border: 1px solid at 5% opacity of `text_primary`.
- State: On focus, the border changes to `accent` and a soft glow of the accent color is applied via shadow.

### Chips & Tags
Used for course categories or interests. Use `background_secondary` with 100px (pill) roundedness. Text should be `label-sm` in `text_secondary`.

### Navigation
Vertical navigation is preferred for the creator dashboard to emphasize the "professional tool" aspect. Icons should be "Outlined" style with a 1.5px stroke width to match the elegant typography.

### Progress Bars
For learning modules, use the `accent` color for the fill and `background_secondary` for the track. The edges must be fully rounded.
